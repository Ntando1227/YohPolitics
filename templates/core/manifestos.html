Manifesto Breakdown

Party promises explained in simple language, with commentary on how they may affect different South Africans.

Example Policy: Free Higher Education Expansion

Plain English Explanation

This means government would increase support for students so that more young people can access university or college.

Possible Benefits

It could reduce financial pressure on students and working-class families.

Possible Criticism

It may require more government spending, which raises questions about funding and implementation.

Demographic Impact

  • Students: More access to education and less debt pressure.
  • Working-class families: Lower education costs.
  • Taxpayers: Possible increased tax burden or budget pressure.
  • Businesses: Long-term benefit from a more skilled workforce.
