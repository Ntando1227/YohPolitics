About Yoh, Politics

Yoh, Politics exists to make South African politics easier to understand without making it shallow.

Mission

We are not here to tell you who to vote for.

Yoh, Politics helps young South Africans understand parties, policies, manifestos, public debates, voter identity, political emotions, and the real-life consequences of political choices.

What makes this different?

Most political platforms explain what parties promise. Yoh, Politics also asks what those promises mean, who they affect, whether they can work, and what kind of South Africa each party is imagining.

Who is it for?

Students, first-time voters, unemployed youth, workers, township entrepreneurs, politically curious citizens, frustrated taxpayers, and anyone trying to understand South African politics beyond slogans.

Important disclaimer

Yoh, Politics is educational and interpretive. It does not represent any political party. It does not replace official party manifestos, legal sources, IEC information, or professional advice.
