Core political tensions

The big questions behind South African politics

Growth or redistribution?

Should South Africa first grow the economy, or directly restructure ownership?

Clean government or radical change?

Is the crisis mainly state failure, or a deeper economic structure that needs disruption?

Safety or social causes?

Is crime mainly a policing crisis, or a symptom of unemployment, inequality, and social collapse?

Borders or solidarity?

How should South Africa balance immigration control, African solidarity, jobs, and public services?
